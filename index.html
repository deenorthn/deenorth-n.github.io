SEO and SMO Techniques

One effective SEO technique is using meta descriptions. Meta descriptions provide a brief summary of a webpage's content and often appear in search engine results pages, helping users decide which link to click (Smith, 2023).

Another SEO method is optimizing page titles. Including keywords in page titles improves the likelihood of a webpage ranking higher in search results (Johnson, 2023).

Finally, ensuring all images have meaningful alt text enhances accessibility and contributes to better SEO by incorporating keywords (Doe, 2023).

For SMO, integrating social media buttons like Facebook's 'Like' button increases visibility and encourages user interaction, boosting engagement (Rohit, 2010).

Another SMO strategy is leveraging hashtags on platforms like Instagram to expand reach and connect with broader audiences (Lockard, 2023).

Lastly, creating shareable and engaging content fosters community participation and drives traffic from social media platforms (Clay, 2023).
