SEO and SMO Techniques

Using meta descriptions is one SEO tactic that works well. Frequently included in search engine results pages, meta descriptions offer a clear overview of a webpage's content and assist readers in selecting which link to click (Backlinko, 2023).

Page title optimization is another SEO strategy. A webpage's chances of appearing higher in search results are increased when keywords are included in the page names (SE Ranking, 2023).

By adding keywords, making sure every image has relevant alt text improves accessibility and helps with SEO (Neilson Marketing Services, 2023).

Including social media buttons improves visibility for SMO and promotes user involvement, which raises engagement (Barker, 2022).

Using hashtags on social media sites like Instagram increases visibility and establishes connections with larger audiences (Lockard, 2023).

Producing interesting and shareable content encourages community involvement and increases social media platform traffic (Clay, 2023).
